General Information

Welcome to OnoAir's help center. Here you can find information about using our flight booking system.

For Users

Booking a Flight

  • Use the "Book a Flight" option from the main menu
  • Filter flights by origin and destination
  • Click the "Book" button next to your desired flight
  • Fill in passenger details in the booking form
  • Verify all information before confirming your booking

Managing Your Bookings

  • View all your bookings in the "My Bookings" section
  • Each booking shows flight details and number of passengers
  • You can see both upcoming and past flights

For Administrators

Managing Flights

  • Add new flights using the "Add Flight" form
  • View all flights in the system
  • Each flight must have a unique flight number
  • Ensure all flight details are accurate before saving

Managing Destinations

  • Add new destinations with required information
  • Destination codes must be unique
  • Provide accurate airport information and website URLs
  • Include destination images for better user experience

Important Notes

  • Flight numbers must follow the standard format (e.g., W61283)
  • All times are shown in local time zones
  • Passenger names must match their passport information
  • Maximum number of passengers per booking may vary by flight
